Crafting Quality Poha Since Generations

At Hubballi Avalakki, we take pride in being one of the most trusted Poha manufacturers in Karnataka. Our journey began with a simple mission – to bring the authentic taste of traditional Avalakki to every household.

Located in the heart of Old Hubli, we have been serving families with fresh, hygienic, and premium quality poha. Every grain of rice is carefully selected and processed to ensure you get nothing but the best.
